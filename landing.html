What is Health Bridge?

Health Bridge is a platform designed to provide essential health services to pregnant mothers and children. It offers features like symptom tracking, vaccination tracking, donation management, and emergency alerts to ensure mothers and children receive timely care. By centralizing healthcare information, Health Bridge aims to support a healthier pregnancy journey.
